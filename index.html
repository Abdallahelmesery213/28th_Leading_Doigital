29th GCC Digital Government and Cities Congress
one of the largest World Digital Government Event

In continuance of conference jubilee (28-Years Celebration), Datamatix in association with global partners, will be extending the celebration in the 28th edition, of successfully organizing one of the largest Global Digital Government and Cities event. On this great occasion Datamatix will host with the participation of the 5 continents, a country from each continent as a guest of honor, for the conference to discuss the best practices and remarkable success and excellence these countries have achieved.

In this context, and in cooperation with global organizations partners, Datamatix with great pleasure presents the 28th GCC Digital Government and Cities Conference on 2-5 October 2023, Dubai, the event aspires to bring together global Digital Government and Cities dignitaries and decision makers to discuss the path to maturity of 5G-6G developments. Middle East Digital Government and Digital Cities Excellence Awards

Recognizing the best practices that are transforming the organizations towards 5G. and the latest Digital Government and Cities achievements and innovative solutions to improve s-Services and sustainable Digital Cities.

The awards goal is to crystallize the best achievements of the Digital institutional transformation, successes of leaders and institutions, and highlight the excellence initiatives and practices in shaping the future of Digital services to develop competitive strategic government orientation in line with the Middle East's competitive objectives. The institute will honor the winners at a special ceremony.
