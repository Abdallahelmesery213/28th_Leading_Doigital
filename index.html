29th Edition, one of the Largest Global
Digital Government and Cities Event

In continuance of conference jubilee (28-Years Celebration), Datamatix in association with global partners, will be extending the celebration in the 28th edition, of successfully organizing one of the largest Global Digital Government and Cities event. On this great occasion Datamatix will host with the participation of the 5 continents, a country from each continent as a guest of honor, for the conference to discuss the best practices and remarkable success and excellence these countries have achieved.

In this context, and in cooperation with global organizations partners, Datamatix with great pleasure presents the 28th GCC Digital Government and Cities Conference on 2-5 October 2023, Dubai, the event aspires to bring together global Digital Government and Cities dignitaries and decision makers to discuss the path to maturity of 5G-6G developments.

The conference is an international platform to discuss and share the new era of technology, organizational transformation, governance policy and latest trends by providing strategic understanding of initiatives for the organizational leadership.
