Datamatix Announcing the 6th Generation of Digital Government and Cities

Datamatix is pleased to announce the unveiling of the 6th Generation of Digital Government and Cities during the 29th GCC Digital Government and Cities Conference in Dubai, UAE, from July 1-4, 2024. This initiative is taken, in collaboration with global ICT and digital consulting firms, aims to empower global digital government and city competitiveness through visionary frameworks aligned with global institutions and governance.

Additionally, the announcement of the 6th Generation of Digital Government and Cities supports the development of international initiatives to promote leadership in sixth-generation applications for digital government and cities. This consideration is vital given the shift towards AI, IoT and cyber security latest technologies to enhance the global competitiveness of digital government and cities.

In this context, Datamatix would like to take this opportunity to thank your organizations for their efforts towards digital government and city development in the past 30 years, as well as the e-Transformational efforts towards advancing the digital government and city competencies. We appreciate the outstanding efforts in shaping the developments of the future as we move towards 2030.

After 30 years of implementing digital government and cities projects, it has become imperative for global governments and authorities to develop new and modern digital transformation strategies based on the science of digital management and governance. This includes reengineering organizational digital and technological structures, optimizing business processes, digital management, and fostering human capital leadership in the era of the digital world.

Additionally, corporate transformation towards artificial intelligence technologies is necessary to support the quality of digital services and develop systems to combat bureaucracy through modern institutional governance and it is also essential to engage the community in supporting the 6G initiative.

In this regard, Datamatix is honored to communicate with the ministers, governors, mayors, leaders, executives and the decision-makers of the United Nation agencies, international official institutions, parliaments, ministries, government organizations, authorities, states, cities, telecom, and the digital transformation teams to join this global event and being part of this international announcement.

Leaders and experts participating will assess organizational competitiveness, strategize digital transformation, governance, and service development, showcase best practices, and propose competitive solutions for the era of the 6th Generation of Digital Government and Cities. Their presence will facilitate global knowledge exchange, opening new horizons for ICT leaders worldwide.

Therefore, conference serves as an important networking opportunity for the organizations and leaders, bringing together digital leaders and experts together to exchange experiences and best practices in digital government and city competencies and provides a platform to address challenges and propose competitive solutions, given the strategic role of your organization in supporting the competitiveness of the digital government and cities services quality, while ensuring staying updated on modern digital developments and future customer happiness.
