Datamatix is Honored to Announce
the 6th Generation of Digital Government and Cities

Datamatix would like to express its sincere appreciation to your organization management for its support in the advancement of the Digital Government and Cities global competencies.

In this context, Datamatix, in collaboration with global ICT and high-tech solutions providers, digital institutions, and in the presence of global digital government and city authorities, experts, leaders, and decision-makers, alongside one of the largest regional and global digital governance and knowledge gatherings, the 29th GCC Digital Government and Cities Conference on May 27th-30th, 2024, in Dubai, UAE, is honored to announce the 6th Generation of Digital Government and Cities within a visionary framework aligned with global digital governance to empower the global digital government and city competitiveness.

In conjunction with the conference the Middle East Excellence Award Institute, with great pleasure will be honoring the “29th Middle East Digital Government and Cities Excellence Awards” winners in the presence of leaders and decision makers for their outstanding leadership.

Datamatix is honored to communicate with your esteemed organization to join as a strategic partner and leaders and decision makers attendance in this international announcement.

Your organizations leader’s participation is a valuable opportunity to assess the regional and global Digital Government and Cities governance and competencies, formulating the digital transformation strategies, re-engineering the corporate, digital quality services development, showcasing their best practices, and suggesting the future competitive solutions to support the transformation in the era of the 6th Generation of Digital Government and Cities. The presence of leaders will enable them to promote the exchange of global digital knowledge and opening a new horizon for digital leaders globally.

The conference serves as an important regional and global networking opportunity for the organizations ad leaders, bringing together leaders to exchange the best practices and knowledge in digital government and city competencies and provides a platform to address challenges and propose solutions, given the strategic role of your organization in supporting the competitiveness of the digital government and cities services quality, while ensuring staying updated on modern digital developments.

Yours sincerely,

The 29th Middle East Digital Government and Cities Excellence Awards

  • 29th Middle East Leading Digital Government and Cities Excellence Award
  • Their Excellencies Quarter Century Digital Leadership Awards
  • Quarter Century Digital Governance and ICT Solution Providers Excellence Awards
  • Quarter Century Digital Governance and ICT Knowledge Leading Awards

Datamatix is pleased to announce the unveiling of the 6th Generation of Digital Government and Cities during the 29th GCC Digital Government and Cities Conference in Dubai, UAE, from May 27th-30th, 2024. This initiative is taken, in collaboration with global ICT and digital consulting firms, aims to empower global digital government and city competitiveness through visionary frameworks aligned with global institutions and governance.

Additionally, the announcement of the 6th Generation of Digital Government and Cities supports the development of international initiatives to promote leadership in sixth-generation applications for digital government and cities. This consideration is vital given the shift towards AI, IoT and cyber security latest technologies to enhance the global competitiveness of digital government and cities.

In this context, Datamatix would like to take this opportunity to thank your organizations for their efforts towards digital government and city development in the past 30 years, as well as the e-Transformational efforts towards advancing the digital government and city competencies. We appreciate the outstanding efforts in shaping the developments of the future as we move towards 2030.

After 30 years of implementing digital government and cities projects, it has become imperative for global governments and authorities to develop new and modern digital transformation strategies based on the science of digital management and governance. This includes reengineering organizational digital and technological structures, optimizing business processes, digital management, and fostering human capital leadership in the era of the digital world.

Additionally, corporate transformation towards artificial intelligence technologies is necessary to support the quality of digital services and develop systems to combat bureaucracy through modern institutional governance and it is also essential to engage the community in supporting the 6G initiative.

In this regard, Datamatix is honored to communicate with the ministers, governors, mayors, leaders, executives and the decision-makers of the United Nation agencies, international official institutions, parliaments, ministries, government organizations, authorities, states, cities, telecom, and the digital transformation teams to join this global event and being part of this international announcement.

Leaders and experts participating will assess organizational competitiveness, strategize digital transformation, governance, and service development, showcase best practices, and propose competitive solutions for the era of the 6th Generation of Digital Government and Cities. Their presence will facilitate global knowledge exchange, opening new horizons for ICT leaders worldwide.

Therefore, conference serves as an important networking opportunity for the organizations and leaders, bringing together digital leaders and experts together to exchange experiences and best practices in digital government and city competencies and provides a platform to address challenges and propose competitive solutions, given the strategic role of your organization in supporting the competitiveness of the digital government and cities services quality, while ensuring staying updated on modern digital developments and future customer happiness.
